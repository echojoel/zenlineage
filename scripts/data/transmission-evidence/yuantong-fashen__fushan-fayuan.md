reducer_notes: |
  C-tier but edge identity remains uncertain. Two distinct "Yuantong" figures
  appear in sources: (1) Fayun Faxiu (法雲法秀), also called Yuantong Faxiu/
  Fayun Yuantong — whose primary teacher was Tianyi Yihuai, not Fushan Fayuan;
  Faxiu only practiced at Fushan Fayuan's mountain. (2) "Yuantong Shen" —
  referenced on the Terebess Touzi Yiqing page as a Yunmen-lineage successor
  whom Fushan Fayuan directed Touzi to study with. The "Yuantong Fashen" in
  the edge list may refer to this second figure (Yuantong Shen), who appears
  in the Yunmen lineage. Fushan Fayuan directing students to Yuantong Shen
  does not constitute a direct teacher–student transmission from Fushan Fayuan
  to Yuantong Fashen; it may be the reverse direction. This edge requires
  Jingde Chuandeng Lu or Wudeng Huiyuan verification to confirm identity
  and direction of transmission.
