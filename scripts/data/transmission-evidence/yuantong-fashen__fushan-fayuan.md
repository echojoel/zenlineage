reducer_notes: |
  C-tier but edge direction and identity remain uncertain. This edge requires
  further verification before any tier upgrade.

  Three "Yuantong" figures appear in sources and must be distinguished:

  1. Fayun Faxiu (法雲法秀, 1027–1090), also called Yuantong Faxiu/Fayun
     Yuantong — whose primary teacher was Tianyi Yihuai, not Fushan Fayuan.
     Faxiu only practiced at Fushan Fayuan's mountain (Longmian), but Fushan
     was not his dharma teacher. This figure is NOT the edge student.

  2. "Yuantong Shen" — referenced on the Terebess Touzi Yiqing page as a
     Yunmen-lineage successor in the 44th generation, whom Fushan Fayuan
     directed Touzi to study with. This suggests Yuantong Shen was a peer or
     senior colleague of Fushan, NOT Fushan's student.

  3. "Yuantong Fashen" — indexed in the Ferguson Scribd chart at E17 with
     Japanese reading Entsū Hōshū. This may be a distinct figure from
     "Yuantong Shen." Without access to the Jingde Chuandeng Lu or Wudeng
     Huiyuan entries, the teacher identity cannot be confirmed.

  Spatial analysis of the Ferguson cuke.com PDF chart does NOT show Fushan
  Fayuan (located at x=998.6, y=495.8) with any node named Yuantong directly
  below it. The node "Xingyang" (x=892.3, y=492.2) appears near Fushan but
  is not Yuantong.

  The edge "yuantong-fashen → fushan-fayuan" cannot be confirmed from
  available sources. It is possible the transmission direction is reversed
  (Fushan studied with or was directed to Yuantong, not vice versa), or that
  "Fashen" is a distinct figure whose teacher remains unidentified.

  RECOMMENDATION: This edge requires verification against the original Wudeng
  Huiyuan or Jingde Chuandeng Lu text before any upgrade. Current Tier C is
  based on the Ferguson chart listing and the spatial proximity in sources,
  but no source explicitly states "Yuantong Fashen is a dharma heir of Fushan
  Fayuan."
