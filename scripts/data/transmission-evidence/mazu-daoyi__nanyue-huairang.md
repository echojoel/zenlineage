Mazu Daoyi (709–788) is universally confirmed as the principal dharma heir of
Nanyue Huairang (677–744). The Mazu Wikipedia article states: "According to
the Transmission of the Lamp, Mazu was a student of Nanyue Huairang at Mount
Heng in Hunan." The famous tile-polishing teaching exchange is recorded there.
Nanyue's Wikipedia article confirms Mazu was his most prominent student who
received transmission after ten years of study. This edge is the root of the
entire Hongzhou school and two of the Five Houses of Chan (Linji and Guiyang).
