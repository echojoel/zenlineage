Dokutan Sosan (1840–1917) received dharma transmission from Tankai Gensho (1811–1898) in the Takuju branch of Rinzai Zen. This is confirmed by Wikipedia, institutional temple sources, and the Terebess lineage database. Dokutan Sosan is notably identified as the 8th generation teacher in the Rinzai line since Hakuin Ekaku and subsequently transmitted to Harada Daiun Sogaku.
