Guizong Zhichang is firmly established as a direct dharma heir of Mazu Daoyi.
The Terebess page quotes from Zen's Chinese Heritage: "Guizong Zhichang (n.d.)
was a disciple of Mazu." The Hongzhou school Wikipedia article records Huangbo
Xiyun's high praise of Guizong among all Mazu's heirs. After leaving Mazu, he
established himself at Guizong Temple on Mount Lu, where he attracted both
monastic and lay students.
