Traditional Chan sources claim Niutou Farong as a dharma heir of Dayi Daoxin (Fourth Patriarch),
founding the Oxhead school. Modern scholarship considers this connection historically tenuous, noting
the Oxhead school may originally have been independent of the main East Mountain Teaching lineage.
