Yozan Keiyo received dharma transmission from Tozen Soshin (1532–1602) in the Myoshinji branch of Rinzai Zen. This is confirmed by Wikipedia's Rinzai lineage template and the Daiyuzenji institutional lineage page, which both place Yozan Keiyo as the link between Tozen Soshin and Gudo Toshoku in the critical Ōtōkan lineage chain.
