Myoki Soseki received dharma transmission from Takuju Kosen (1760–1833), the founder of the Takuju branch of post-Hakuin Rinzai Zen. This places Myoki Soseki as the direct successor to Takuju Kosen in the lineage that continues through Karyo Zuika, Tankai Gensho, and Dokutan Sosan. Confirmed by institutional temple pages and academic reference sources.
