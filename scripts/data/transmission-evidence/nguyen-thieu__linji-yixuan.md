Nguyên Thiều (1648-1728) brought the Linji (Lâm Tế) school from China to Vietnam in 1665,
founding the Nguyên-Thiêu school. His lineage traces back to Linji Yixuan through approximately
30+ generations. This edge represents school-founder lineage affiliation.
