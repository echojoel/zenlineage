Nyogen Senzaki studied under Soyen Shaku at Engaku-ji but never received formal dharma transmission (inka). Three independent sources confirm both the teacher-student relationship and the absence of formal transmission. Only Tetsuo Sokatsu is listed as Soyen Shaku's dharma heir. This edge represents a lineage-influence connection, not a formal Rinzai transmission. Tier C.
