Nyogen Senzaki was a devoted student of Soyen Shaku at Engaku-ji but did not receive formal dharma transmission (inka). Multiple independent sources confirm the teacher-student relationship while also noting that Senzaki—alongside D.T. Suzuki—was explicitly not a formal dharma heir. Only Tetsuo Sokatsu is listed as Soyen Shaku's dharma heir on Wikipedia. The edge is real as a lineage-influence connection but does not represent a formal Rinzai inka transmission.
