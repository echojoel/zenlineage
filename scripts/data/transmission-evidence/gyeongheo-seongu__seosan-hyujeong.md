  Gyeongheo Seongu (鏡虛惺牛, 1849–1912) traces his dharma lineage to Seosan Hyujeong (Hyujŏng, 西山休靜) as his 12th-generation ancestral master, not as a direct teacher. The transmission spans approximately twelve generations from Seosan to Gyeongheo via intermediate masters including Yongam and Hwanseong Chian. Gyeongheo's immediate teacher was Manhwa (Man Hwa), from whom he received formal transmission and the dharma name Kyong Ho.
