Taigu Puyu (Korean: Taego Bou, 1301-1382) received dharma transmission from Shiwu Qinggong
(Stonehouse, 1272-1352) in 1347 and became the first master of the Linji (Rinzai) school in Korea,
where he integrated it with the existing Korean Seon tradition.
