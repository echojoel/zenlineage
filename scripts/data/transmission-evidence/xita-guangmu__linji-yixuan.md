Xita Guangmu appears in Chan lineage charts as a 12th-generation master consistent with direct studentship under Linji Yixuan. Terebess explicitly names Xita Guangmu as Zifu Rubao's teacher, placing Xita one generation after Linji. However, both Terebess and Polish Wikipedia classify the pair within the Guiyang school rather than purely the Linji school, which warrants human review.
