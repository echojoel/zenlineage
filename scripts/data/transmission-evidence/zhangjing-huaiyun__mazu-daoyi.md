Zhangjing Huaiyun (also Zhangjing Huaihui, 756–815/816) is confirmed as a
dharma heir of Mazu Daoyi. The Terebess page states explicitly: "He began
study under Mazu and later became his Dharma heir." He was among the first of
Mazu's students to establish the Hongzhou school in the Tang capital Chang'an,
bringing considerable prestige to the lineage.
