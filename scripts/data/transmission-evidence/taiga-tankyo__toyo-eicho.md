Taiga Tankyo (d. 1518) received dharma transmission from Toyo Eicho (1428/9–1504) in the Myoshinji branch of Rinzai Zen. This is confirmed by Wikipedia's Rinzai lineage template and an institutional temple lineage page. The Toyo Eicho → Taiga Tankyo → Koho Genkun succession is a well-documented sequence in the medieval Myoshinji lineage descending from Kanzan Egen.
