Longya Judun was a direct dharma heir of Dongshan Liangjie, the co-founder of the Caodong school. He studied with Dongshan for eight years and received enlightenment through Dongshan's teaching. He also studied with Linji Yixuan and Cuiwei Wuxue to broaden his understanding.
