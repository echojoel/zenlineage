Yanguan Qian (died 842) is attested as a dharma heir of Mazu Daoyi. The
Terebess master list states "A Dharma-heir of Mazu. A posthumous title was
Wukong." The Hongzhou school Wikipedia article groups him among Mazu's
disciples who formed a community in Zhejiang. He is recorded in Wudeng Vol. 3.
