Jingqing Daofu (ca. 863–937) is confirmed as a direct dharma heir of Xuefeng
Yicun. He is Yunmen Wenyan's dharma sibling under Xuefeng. The Xuefeng
Wikipedia article lists him in the lineage chart as a direct successor. The
WWZC master list gives an explicit "student and dharma-heir of Xuefeng Yicun."
A famous teaching exchange when Jingqing arrived at Xuefeng's monastery —
being directed to "Enter there" via the sound of water — is recorded in
multiple sources.
