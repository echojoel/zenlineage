Sansheng Huiran was one of Linji Yixuan's direct dharma heirs and is known for compiling an early version of the *Linji lu*. He was present at Linji's death and received the "True Dharma Eye." Named in both Wikipedia and the Encyclopedia of Buddhism among Linji's small circle of students.
