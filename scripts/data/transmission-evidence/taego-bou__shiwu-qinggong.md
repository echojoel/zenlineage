  Taego Bou (太古普愚, 1301–1382) traveled to Yuan China in 1346 and received dharma transmission from Shiwu Qinggong (石屋清珙, 1272–1352) in 1347. This is the primary transmission that unified Korean Buddhism under the Taego lineage.
