Wuzhun Shifan was the direct dharma heir of Poan Zuxian, continuing the Yangqi branch of the Linji school through the 20th to 21st patriarchal generation. Wuzhun's students include Xueyan Zuqin and the Japanese monk Enni Ben'en.
