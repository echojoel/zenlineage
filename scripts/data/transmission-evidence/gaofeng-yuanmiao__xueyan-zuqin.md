Gaofeng Yuanmiao received dharma transmission from Xueyan Zuqin. Their encounter, in which Xueyan gave Gaofeng the huatou "where is your master?", is one of the signature events in Yuan-dynasty Chan history. Gaofeng's lineage continued through Zhongfeng Mingben.
