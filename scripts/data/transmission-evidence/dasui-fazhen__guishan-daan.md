Dasui Fazhen (878–963) is uniformly identified as a dharma heir of Changqing
Da'an, who is the same person as Guishan Da'an. The Terebess master list
explicitly states "A Dharma-heir of Changqing Daan, grandchild in Dharma to
Baizhang." A recorded enlightenment encounter between teacher and student is
preserved on the Terebess Dasui page. Multiple sources confirm he visited
Changqing Da'an at Mt. Gui as his final and decisive teacher.
