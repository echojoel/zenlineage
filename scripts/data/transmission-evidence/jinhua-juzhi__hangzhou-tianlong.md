Hangzhou Tianlong transmitted dharma to Jinhua Juzhi through a single gesture — raising one finger. Juzhi spent his entire teaching career using this same gesture, declaring on his deathbed that he had "inherited Tianlong's one-finger Zen." This transmission is commemorated in Case 3 of the Gateless Gate.
