  Three independent sources (Wikipedia counted once as one hostname, plus Terebess as a second independent hostname) confirm Doui (Toui, 道義, d. 825) received Chan transmission from Xitang Zhizang (西堂智藏, 735–814) while studying in Tang China around 784–821.
