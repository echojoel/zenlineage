Sixin Wuxin (1044-1115) appears in multiple Chan lineage charts as a member of the Baiyun Shouduan dharma family. Some sources attribute his transmission to Huitang Zuxin (a dharma brother) rather than directly to Baiyun Shouduan. Human review recommended to confirm the precise teacher.
