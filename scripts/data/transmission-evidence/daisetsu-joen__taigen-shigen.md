Daisetsu Jo'en (1797–1855) is confirmed as a dharma heir of Taigen Shigen (1768–1837) in the Inzan Rinzai lineage by the Ciolek academic lineage chart and Daiyuzenji institutional chart. The Wikipedia Gisan article listing Daisetsu as Gisan's student likely indicates co-study at the same training center rather than a different transmission line. Tier B.
