Daisetsu Joen (1797–1855) is listed in multiple sources as a dharma heir within the Taigen Shigen / Inzan lineage, but there is an apparent conflict: Wikipedia's Gisan Zenrai article lists Daisetsu Joen as Gisan's student, while other lineage charts position him as Taigen Shigen's direct heir (placing him one generation earlier, as Gisan's dharma-sibling rather than dharma-grandchild). Human review recommended to confirm which teacher formally transmitted to Daisetsu Joen.
