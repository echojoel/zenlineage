Myoan Eisai (1141–1215) received direct dharma transmission from Xu'an Huaichang (c.1125–1195), the 8th-generation Huanglong teacher — not from Huanglong Huinan (d. 1069) directly. Three sources confirm this lineage chain. The edge myoan-eisai → huanglong-huinan represents school affiliation (Eisai introduced the Huanglong line to Japan) rather than direct one-step transmission. Tier C. Edge semantics should be reviewed.
