Panshan Baoji (720–814) is confirmed as a dharma heir of Mazu Daoyi. The
Terebess page states he was "a disciple of the great Zen master Mazu Daoyi"
and that Mazu confirmed his awakening after a profound experience at a funeral
procession. His student Puhua's Wikipedia article also calls him a dharma heir
of Panshan Baoji, confirming the transmission chain through him.
