Guannan Daochang is identified in Chan genealogical records as a tenth-generation descendant in the
Shitou Xiqian line. No source confirms a direct one-hop teacher-student relationship; this edge may
represent lineage affiliation through intermediate masters. The direct teacher has not been positively
identified in English-language sources.
