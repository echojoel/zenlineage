Nanpu Shaoming (Nanpo Shōmyō / Daiō Kokushi, 1235–1308) received dharma transmission from Xutang Zhiyu (1185–1269) in China in 1265, establishing the Ōtōkan lineage upon returning to Japan in 1267. This is among the most pivotal transmissions in Rinzai history, as the Ōtōkan lineage (named for Nanpo, Shuho Myocho/Daitō, and Kanzan Egen) is today the only surviving dharma-transmission line in Japanese Rinzai Zen.
