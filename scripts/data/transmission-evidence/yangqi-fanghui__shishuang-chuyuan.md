Shishuang Chuyuan was the direct dharma teacher of Yangqi Fanghui. The Yangqi school, one of the two major branches of the Song-dynasty Linji lineage (the other being Huanglong), descends from this transmission. All modern Japanese Rinzai Zen traces back through the Yangqi line.
