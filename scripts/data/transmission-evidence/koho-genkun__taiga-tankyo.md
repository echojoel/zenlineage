Koho Genkun (d. 1524) received dharma transmission from Taiga Tankyo (d. 1518) within the Myoshinji branch of Rinzai Zen. This link is confirmed by Wikipedia's Rinzai lineage template and corroborated by the Daiyuzenji institutional lineage page, placing them as successive generations in the Kanzan Egen → Toyo Eicho → Taiga Tankyo → Koho Genkun chain.
