Nakagawa Soen (1907–1984) received inka (dharma transmission) from Yamamoto Gempo (1866–1961) and succeeded him as abbot of Ryutaku-ji in 1951/1958 (sources differ on exact date of succession vs. transmission). This is confirmed by Wikipedia, Hollow Bones Zen, and Tricycle. Yamamoto Gempo is described as a twentieth-century Hakuin-like figure in Rinzai history.
