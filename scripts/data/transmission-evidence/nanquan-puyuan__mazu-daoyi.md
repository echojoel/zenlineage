Nanquan Puyuan (748–835) is universally recorded as a dharma heir of Mazu
Daoyi. His Wikipedia article states: "He was a student and Dharma successor
of Master Mazu Daoyi (709–788)." His enlightenment under Mazu in 795 is noted,
after which he retired to Mount Nanquan. He later became teacher of Zhaozhou
Congshen, confirming his position in the Tang Chan transmission chain.
