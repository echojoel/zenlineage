Shaku Sokatsu (Tetsuo Sokatsu) received formal dharma transmission (inka) from Soyen Shaku at age 29, making him Soyen Shaku's sole listed dharma heir. This is confirmed by Wikipedia's article on both Soyen Shaku and Tetsuo Sokatsu, as well as multiple independent sangha sources. He subsequently founded Ryōbō Kai and extended the lineage to Sokei-an and Ruth Fuller Sasaki's line.
