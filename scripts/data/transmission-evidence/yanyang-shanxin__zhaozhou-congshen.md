Yanyang Shanxin is listed in the Terebess Asia Online master list as "One of
two Dharma-heirs of Zhaozhou." The Zhaozhou Wikipedia article confirms that his
lineage was very limited ("died out quickly"), consistent with only two recorded
successors. Yanyang Shanxin is the most documented of those two heirs in
English-language Chan resources.
