Ingen Ryuki (Yinyuan Longqi, 1592-1673) was a Linji school heir who brought Chan Buddhism to Japan
and founded the Obaku school of Zen. He received dharma transmission from Feiyin Tongrong. This
edge represents his lineage affiliation to Linji Yixuan through many intermediate generations.
