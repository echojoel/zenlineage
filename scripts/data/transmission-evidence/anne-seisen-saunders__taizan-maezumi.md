Anne Seisen Saunders studied with Taizan Maezumi but received formal dharma transmission from
Tetsugen Bernard Glassman (Maezumi's heir) in 1996, after Maezumi's death in 1995. She is a
member of the White Plum Asanga in the Maezumi lineage.
