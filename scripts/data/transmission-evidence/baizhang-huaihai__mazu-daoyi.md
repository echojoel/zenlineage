Baizhang Huaihai (720–814) is one of the most celebrated dharma heirs of Mazu
Daoyi. The Wikipedia article states he "served as his attendant for twenty years."
The Hongzhou school Wikipedia article calls him "a dharma heir of Mazu" and
notes he "later came to be seen as Mazu's most important disciple." Two major
Chan schools — Linji and Guiyang — descend from Baizhang, making this one of
the most consequential edges in the Nanyue lineage.
