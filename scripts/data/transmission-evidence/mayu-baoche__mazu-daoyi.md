Mayu Baoche (also written Magu Baoche) is listed on Terebess as "one of Mazu
Daoyi's heirs." An awakening exchange while walking with Mazu is documented:
Mazu confirmed his understanding when Mayu said "See the water!" The Terebess
master list entry states: "Magu Baoche (n.d.): A practitioner of Mazu." He was
active in the northern provinces (Shaanxi and Shanxi) as the first abbot of
the monastery at Mount Mayu.
