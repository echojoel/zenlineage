CONFLICT: All available sources place Baoen Xuanze in the Fayan Wenyi lineage,
not Yunmen Wenyan's. The Terebess master list states explicitly: "Baoen Xuanze
(9th–10th Century): A Dharma-heir of Fayan." Multiple independent sources
document his enlightenment exchange with Fayan Wenyi: he first studied under
Qingfeng, then received dharma from Fayan when asking "What is the self of this
student?" Fayan replied "The fire boy comes seeking fire." Zero sources found
naming Yunmen Wenyan as Baoen Xuanze's teacher. The edge
yunmen-wenyan->baoen-xuanze is very likely erroneous; the correct teacher
is Fayan Wenyi.
