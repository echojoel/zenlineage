Kono Bukai is attested as the strict abbot of Nanzenji under whom Shibayama Zenkei trained for over ten years (from 1916). The lineage context places Kono Bukai/Mukai in the Tankai Gensho → Dokutan Sosan line, but it is unclear whether the teacher of Kono Bukai was Dokutan Sosan or Tankai Gensho directly. Disambiguation of "Kono Bukai" vs. "Mukai Koryo / Kono Mukai" is needed before this edge can be confirmed with confidence.
