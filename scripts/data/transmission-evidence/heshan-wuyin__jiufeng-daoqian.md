Heshan Wuyin received dharma transmission from Jiufeng Daoqian. He is best known for his teaching method of "knowing how to beat a drum," which appears in Blue Cliff Record Case 44. His initial ordination was under Xuefeng Yicun, but Jiufeng Daoqian was his dharma teacher.
