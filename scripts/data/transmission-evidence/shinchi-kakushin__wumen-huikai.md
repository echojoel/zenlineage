Shinchi Kakushin (1207–1298) received formal dharma transmission from Wumen Huikai (1183–1260) during his study in China, along with a robe, portrait, and handwritten copy of the Mumonkan. This is confirmed by Wikipedia, the Terebess reference database, and the Dōgen Institute. Kakushin returned to Japan and founded the Hōtō lineage, making him the conduit for Wumen's Chan/Rinzai teachings to Japan.
