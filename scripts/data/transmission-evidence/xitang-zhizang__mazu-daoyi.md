Xitang Zhizang (735–814) is confirmed as a direct dharma heir of Mazu Daoyi.
The Terebess master list states this explicitly. The Hongzhou school Wikipedia
article confirms that after Mazu's death, "Xitang Zhizang became the leader
of Kaiyuan monastery." His Korean dharma heirs Jilin Daoyi and Hongshe were
fundamental in establishing the Nine Mountains of Korean Chan.
