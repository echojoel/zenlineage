Nanyin Shourou (Nanyuan Huiyong, d. 930/952) was the primary dharma heir of Xinghua Cunjiang (830–888) in the Linji/Rinzai Chan lineage. This is confirmed by Terebess and Wikipedia's Linji Yixuan article. Nanyuan Huiyong is identified as the most important third-generation teacher of the Linji school and the critical link through whom all later Rinzai lineages descend.
