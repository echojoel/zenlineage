Zhongyi Hongen appears in Chan lineage charts among Mazu Daoyi's dharma heirs,
listed alongside Baizhang Huaihai, Xitang, Panshan, and Nanquan Puyuan. However,
almost no English-language biographical information is available. This is a
minimally documented figure; human review is recommended to verify the correct
identity from Chinese-language lamp records.
