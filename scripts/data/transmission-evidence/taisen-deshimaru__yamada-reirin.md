  Tier B: four independent sources across three hostnames (Wikipedia ×2,
  AZI institutional, Méditation Zen sangha) all confirm 1970 as the year
  Yamada Reirin formally conferred shihō on Deshimaru. The context is
  important: Deshimaru had claimed a deathbed entrustment from Kōdō Sawaki
  in 1965 but that was never registered with Sōtōshū; Yamada Reirin's 1970
  transmission is the institutionally recognised shihō that brought Deshimaru
  inside the regular Sōtōshū succession — making it the "secondary" edge in
  graph terms (Sawaki is primary as spiritual master) but the
  institutionally load-bearing one for the downstream European lineage.
