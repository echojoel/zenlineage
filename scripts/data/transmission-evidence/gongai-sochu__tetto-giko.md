Gongai Sochu (1315–1390) became the principal dharma heir of Tetto Giko (1295–1369) after achieving enlightenment during a 90-day training session at Daitokuji. This is confirmed by multiple Wikipedia articles, Wikiquote, and an institutional Rinzai lineage page. Tetto Giko served as abbot for 31 years and is a key figure in the Ōtōkan lineage descending from Shuho Myocho.
