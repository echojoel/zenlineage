Gisan Zenrai received dharma transmission from Taigen Shigen in the Inzan line of Rinzai Zen. After Taigen Shigen died in 1837, Gisan became abbot of Sōgen-ji and went on to teach many disciples, including Tekisui Giboku, Imakita Kosen, and Daisetsu Joen. This transmission is confirmed across Wikipedia, institutional, and sangha sources.
