Ryoen Genseki (1842/43–1918/19) received dharma transmission from Tekisui Giboku (1822–1899) in the Inzan line of Rinzai Zen. The German Zen-Guide database explicitly records the transmission, corroborated by the Daiyuzenji institutional lineage and a sangha lineage page. Tekisui Giboku was himself a dharma heir of Gisan Zenrai and abbot of Tenryu-ji.
