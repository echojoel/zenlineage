Dongshan Shouchu is confirmed as a dharma heir of Yunmen Wenyan. His Wikipedia
stub explicitly states he was "an heir to Yunmen Wenyan." Yunmen's Wikipedia
article states that "Yunmen was succeeded as abbot by Dongshan Shouchu,"
confirming both lineage and institutional succession. He carried Yunmen's
transmission forward at Yunmen Monastery after the master's death.
