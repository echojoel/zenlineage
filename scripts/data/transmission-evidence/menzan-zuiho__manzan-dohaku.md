  The Japanese Wikipedia article on Menzan Zuiho explicitly names Manzan Dohaku as his primary teacher (dharma lineage parent). Both collaborated in the Soto reform movement of the Edo period, with Menzan producing over 100 scholarly works extending and systematizing Manzan's reform agenda. The English Wikipedia article on Menzan confirms his dates (1683-1769) and scholarly prominence but lists his teacher as "Dōgen" (apparently meaning his spiritual lineage root rather than direct teacher). The Japanese Wikipedia is more accurate in identifying Manzan Dohaku as his direct teacher. Both sources resolve to the Wikipedia hostname.
