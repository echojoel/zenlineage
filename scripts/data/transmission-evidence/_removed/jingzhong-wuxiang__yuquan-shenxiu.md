  The edge yuquan-shenxiu → jingzhong-wuxiang is incorrect. Multiple sources
  confirm Jingzhong Wuxiang (Kim Hwasang, 684–762) was a Korean Chan master

  Zhishen (609–702) → Zizhou Chuji (669–736) → Jingzhong Wuxiang (684–762).

  Wuzhu describes Zhishen as "grandfather-in-dharma to Wuxiang," confirming
  the two-generation distance. The correct teacher of Wuxiang is Zizhou Chuji
