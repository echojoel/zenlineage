  Nishiari Bokusan (西有穆山, 1821-1910) is a major Soto figure whose lineage traces back to Gasan Joseki through multiple generations. His direct predecessor/teacher was Ansu Taigen (安住大源). The lineage chain connecting Nishiari to Gasan Joseki runs through Menzan Zuiho, Gesshū Sōko, and several other masters. This is a lineage-chain connection (multiple generations removed), not a direct teacher-student relationship with Gasan Joseki.
