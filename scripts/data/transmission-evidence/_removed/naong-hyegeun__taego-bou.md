reducer_notes: |
  DOWNGRADED TO TIER D. Multiple independent sources now consistently refute a teacher-student relationship between Naong Hyegeun (懶翁慧勤, 1320–1376) and Taego Bou (1301–1382). The evidence is unambiguous: (1) Naong's primary dharma transmissions came from two Chinese masters — Pingshan Chulin (18th Patriarch of the Linji School) and the Indian master Zhikong Zhijian — not from Taego Bou; (2) all sources describe Naong and Taego Bou as "contemporaries and friends," never as teacher and student; (3) the Terebess article on Taego Bou lists his major disciples (Hwanam Honsu, Mogam Chanyeong, Myoeom Joi) and Naong Hyegeun is absent; (4) the Jogye Order institutional source confirms Naong's two actual teachers without mentioning Taego Bou in that capacity. This edge should be removed or replaced with a "colleague/contemporary" relationship tag.
