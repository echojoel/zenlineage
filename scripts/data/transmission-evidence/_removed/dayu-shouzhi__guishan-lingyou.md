  CONFIRMED WRONG EDGE. Multiple independent sources establish that Dayu Shouzhi
  (大愚守芝, also known as "Cuiyan") belongs to the Linji school, not the Guiyang
  school, and has no documented connection to Guishan Lingyou.

  Confirmed teacher: Fenyang Shanzhao (947–1024), a Linji lineage master.
  - Polish Wikipedia on Dayu Shouzhi: "Fenyang Shanzhao appeared in the area and
    Dayu went to listen to him. After this event, Dayu became his student and
    ultimately received the Dharma transmission of Linji." The article places
    Dayu Shouzhi at generation 44/17 under Fenyang Shanzhao (43/16).
  - Polish Wikipedia on Fenyang Shanzhao lists Dayu Shouzhi explicitly among
    Fenyang's dharma heirs at generation 44/17.
  - Ferguson Lineage Chart (cuke.com / Scribd / pdfcoffee) shows Dayu Shouzhi
    "Cuiyan" (Ta-yü Shou-chih / Daigu Shushi) in the Linji school chart under
    Langye Huijue / Fushan Fayuan / Ciming Chuyuan branch — entirely separate
    from the Guiyang school (Guishan Lingyou) section.
  - Terebess page on Huanglong Huinan records that Cuiyan Wenyue "received
    formal transmission from Dayu Shouzhi" and then served under Huanglong
    Huinan — placing Dayu firmly in the Song Linji (Huanglong) world.
  - The angelfire.com "Zen Ancestors" page places Dayu Shouzhi in the 17th
    generation alongside Langye Huijue, Shishuang Chuyuan (Ciming), Fushan
    Fayuan — all Linji masters.

  Guishan Lingyou (771–853) was a Tang dynasty Guiyang school founder; Dayu
  Shouzhi lived in the Song dynasty (dates uncertain, but his student Yunfeng
  Wenyue died 1062). The chronological gap alone makes a direct transmission
  impossible. Guishan Lingyou's documented heirs are Yangshan Huiji and
  Xiangyan Zhixian; no source names Dayu Shouzhi among them.

  This edge is a confirmed misidentification — likely a confusion with the Tang
  master Gaoan Dayu (高安大愚), who was a Mazu-lineage figure, or simply an
  erroneous data entry. The edge should be removed from the lineage graph.
  Tier D maintained. Recommend deletion from the dataset.
