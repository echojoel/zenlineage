reducer_notes: |
  CONFIRMED DATA ERROR. Huanglong Huinan died in 1069 and belonged to the Linji/Huanglong
  school (approximately generation 11 in the lineage chain from Bodhidharma). Dahong
  Zuzheng is placed in the 23rd generation in Ferguson's lineage chart. A direct teacher–
  student relationship spanning 12 generations is impossible.

  The correct direct teacher of Dahong Zuzheng appears to be Xuedou Zhijian
  (祖菴智鑑, 1105–1192), a Caodong school master and student of Tiantong Zongjue
  (who was himself a student of Zhenxie Qingliao). This places Dahong Zuzheng firmly
  in the Caodong lineage, not the Huanglong lineage.

  The Academia.edu source confirms Dahong Zuzheng was affiliated with "the Dharma-heirs
  of Huanglong Huinan," meaning he studied within the Huanglong school tradition but was
  not a direct heir of Huanglong Huinan himself.

  The edge as coded (huanglong-huinan → dahong-zuzheng) is wrong. The lineage data should
  be corrected to reflect Xuedou Zhijian as the direct teacher, or the edge should be
  removed entirely. Tier D is correct and permanent.
