reducer_notes: |
  CONFIRMED WRONG EDGE. Dagui Muzhe's actual teacher is Changshui Zixuan
  (d. 1038), not Furong Daokai (1043–1118). The edge should be removed
  from the lineage graph.

  The Polish Wikipedia on Fenyang Shanzhao provides the direct lineage chain:
    Fenyang Shanzhao (43/16)
      → Langye Huijue (44/17)
        → Changshui Zixuan (45/18, d. 1038)
          → Dagui Muzhe (46/19, d. 1095)

  This places Dagui Muzhe firmly in the Linji school lineage (via Fenyang
  Shanzhao), not in the Caodong school lineage of Furong Daokai.

  Furong Daokai (1043–1118) was a Caodong master whose teacher was Touzi
  Yiqing. He cannot have been Dagui Muzhe's teacher because:
  1. Dagui Muzhe (d. 1095) predeceased Furong Daokai by decades.
  2. All lineage charts (Ferguson, Polish Wikipedia on Fenyang Shanzhao,
     herit.pdf) place Dagui Muzhe under Changshui Zixuan, not Furong Daokai.

  The herit.pdf table of contents listing (Zen's Chinese Heritage) shows
  Dagui Muche in the 19th-generation section alongside Danxia Zichun and
  Baofeng Weizhao (confirmed Furong Daokai heirs), but the Ferguson index
  (Scribd/pdfcoffee) encodes Dagui Muzhe as "E19" while Danxia Zichun and
  the other Furong heirs carry different codes — the shared generation-level
  grouping in herit.pdf appears to be typographic, not a teacher assignment.
  The Polish Wikipedia lineage tree is unambiguous about Changshui Zixuan.

  Note: the reddit r/zens lineage wiki also shows the chain
  "Langye Huijue → Changshui Zixuan → Dagui Muzhe" confirming the same.

  Previous Tier C rating was based on indirect sources that only confirmed
  Furong Daokai had many heirs, not that Dagui Muzhe was one of them.
  Downgraded to Tier D (contradicted). Recommend deletion from dataset.
