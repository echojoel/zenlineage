  WRONG EDGE — downgraded to D. Multiple sources confirm that Umpo Zenjo
  and Gudo Toshoku were respected contemporaries within the Myoshinji
  Rinzai tradition, but Gudo Toshoku was NOT Umpo Zenjo's dharma teacher.
  Evidence:

  1. The Wikipedia Bankei article shows Umpo RECOMMENDED Bankei go see Gudo —
     a peer collegial referral, not the behavior of a student referring his
     own teacher.
  2. Buddhism: The Way of Emptiness states that Gudo was "enlightened under
     master Nankei Sogaku, the same master from whom Umpo Zenjo later
     received his inka" — they shared a dharma grandfather but neither was
     the other's teacher.
  3. No source found anywhere stating Gudo Toshoku transmitted dharma to
     Umpo Zenjo.

  The correct lineage relationship: Umpo Zenjo's dharma teacher was
  Nankei Sogaku (or a student of Nankei), not Gudo Toshoku. The edge
  as currently encoded (umpo-zenjo student of gudo-toshoku) is contradicted
  by the available evidence. Data correction required.
