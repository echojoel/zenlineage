  Naong Hyegeun (懶翁慧勤, 1320–1376) and Taego Bou (1301–1382) were contemporaries and colleagues who both traveled to Yuan China to receive Linji transmission. Naong received his primary transmission from Pingshan Chulin (and also met the Indian master Zhikong Zhijian) in 1347. Sources describe the two as "contemporaries and friends" rather than in a strict teacher-student relationship.
