  Naong Hyegeun (懶翁慧勤, 1320–1376) and Taego Bou (1301–1382) were contemporaries and colleagues who both traveled to Yuan China to receive Linji transmission. Naong received his primary transmission from Pingshan Chulin (and also met the Indian master Zhikong Zhijian) in 1347. Sources describe the two as "contemporaries and friends" rather than in a strict teacher-student relationship. The Terebess source on Taego Bou lists his major disciples but does not name Naong Hyegeun among them. Adding Terebess as a second hostname brings this to two independent sources; however, the direct teacher-student edge remains contested and unconfirmed in fetched sources.
