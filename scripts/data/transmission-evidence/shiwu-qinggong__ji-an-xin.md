Shiwu Qinggong (1272-1352) received dharma transmission from Ji-an Xin (Chi-an) after being referred by Gaofeng's community. Their teacher-student relationship is confirmed by Wikipedia's biography of Shiwu and by academic literature on Yuan-dynasty Chan.
