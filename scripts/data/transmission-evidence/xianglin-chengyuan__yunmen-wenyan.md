Xianglin Chengyuan (908-987) was a dharma heir of Yunmen Wenyan, having served as his personal
attendant (jisha) for 18 years. He is one of the most important successors in the Yunmen school
lineage, transmitting the dharma to Zhimen Guangzuo.
