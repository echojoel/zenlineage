Bankei Yotaku (1622–1693) became the formal dharma heir of Umpo Zenjo at Zuiō-ji, with the transmission completed posthumously per Umpo's dying wish. Bankei had ordained under Umpo at age 16 and Umpo confirmed his initial enlightenment; formal transmission came years later. Bankei also received transmission from the Chinese master Dosha Chogen. Confirmed by Wikipedia and multiple independent sources.
