Brand Direction

Three visual identities — each minimal and meditative, but with a different soul

A
Ink Wash — Sumi-e Inspired
Inspired by sumi-e ink painting. Lines vary in weight and opacity like brush strokes. Warm stone and paper tones. Masters are ink dots that bloom on hover. The feel: contemplative, hand-crafted, timeless. Colors: warm greys, aged paper, ink brown, moss green.
B
Stone Garden — Karesansui
Inspired by karesansui (dry rock gardens). Masters are stones of varying sizes. Connections are raked paths. The space between matters as much as the elements. The feel: austere, spacious, geometric. Colors: sand, stone, moss, minimal.
C
Paper & Thread — Washi Inspired
Inspired by washi paper and red thread (en-musubi, the thread of connection). Clean open space. Thin golden threads connect masters. Double-circle nodes suggest ensō. The feel: delicate, breathing, warm. Colors: warm white, gold thread, soft ink, paper texture.
