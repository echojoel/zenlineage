Core Pages

Lineage Explorer

/lineage

THE core experience. Full-screen animated flow visualization. Zoom, pan, time-scrub through 2,500 years. Click any master to open their profile in a sidebar. Filter by school, era, region.

Master Profile

/masters/[slug]

Rich page: biography, dharma names in all languages, teacher & students, key teachings, temple affiliations, artwork, map of locations. Link back to their position in the lineage tree.

biography multilingual names mini lineage teachings

School Page

/schools/[slug]

History of a school (Rinzai, Soto, etc.), its founding, key practices, notable masters, sub-branches. Embedded mini-lineage showing this school's tree.

history practices sub-branches

Teachings & Koans

/teachings

Browse by collection (Blue Cliff Record, Mumonkan, Shōbōgenzō), by master, or by theme. Each koan/teaching links to its author's profile.

collections search themes

Temple Map

/temples

Interactive map of temples and monasteries. Filter by school, era, status (active/historical). Click for details, history, and connected masters.
